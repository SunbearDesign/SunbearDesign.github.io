Master Thesis Project

Overview

For my master thesis project, I created a system to aid people in reducing their spending on electricity or the environmental impact of their electricity usage through shifting. Shifting refers to the idea of impacting oneâ€™s electricity usage positively, not by reducing total usage, but by shifting usage to other times with a lower price and/or an abundance of renewable energy.
My primary roles were: Planning, conducting, and analysing workshops, deployments, and interviews, concept creation, and creation of wireframes and prototypes.

Process & Product

Prior to the project we completed a pre-project focused on user research. For this I planned, conducted, and analysed an inspiration card workshop, as well as a co-design session with potential users. Based on this, we created a prototype which could be used to test the basic concept of the system and to gain a deeper understanding of how the system impacts the practice of electricity shifting.
An interactable version of this prototype can be seen under the project road map. This deployment primarily uncovered a number of nice-to-have features. To further understand these needs, we conducted three separate workshops with two participants each. I planned, conducted, and analysed these as well. Based on the findings of these we created a final prototype.
